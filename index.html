Why BiasOps?

BiasOps transforms fairness from a checkbox into infrastructure. Detect, mitigate, and log bias—before it reaches production.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Bias Detection across protected groups
  • Config-as-Code for fairness enforcement
  • Evidential Learning for uncertainty-aware bias scoring
  • Repair Agent to reroute, explain, or alert unfair predictions
  • Bias Ledger: Immutable audit log
  • Policy Marketplace: Templates for EU AI Act, NYC 144, and more

Who It's For

  • ML & MLOps Teams in Regulated Industries
  • AI Compliance Officers
  • Startups building production AI systems
